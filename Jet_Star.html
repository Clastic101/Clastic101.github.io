Jet Star

Help a lost star find his way home! Fly through blackholes and dodge
meteoroids and astroids as you find the path back to your home!

[Android] Programmed in JavaScript. Made with Unity.

Jet Star is a game my brother and I made back in 2014 as a small project that warmed us up for our larger project "Dodgeball Blitz". All of the art he made and I did all of the programming. Most music and sound FX were taken from free sources online. It took about 6 weeks to make what you see. If you are interested in playing the game, please see the link below to download the SPK.
